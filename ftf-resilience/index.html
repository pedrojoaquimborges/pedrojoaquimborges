Resilience
Forbidden to Fly

Resillience is an extended-play record I released as a guitarist and member of Forbidden to Fly in 2016. The music is best described straight up metalcore that thrives on tight, heavy riffs, big drums and poppy hooks.

I did editorial/graphic design for the record’s printed material.

  • Guitar and Co-Production
  • Released January 2016
  • Independent Release
