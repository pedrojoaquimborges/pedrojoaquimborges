Three Poems for an Early Spring
Pedro Joaquim Borges

Three Poems for Early Spring is a postcard from a short trip to Lisbon. Somewhere between music, sound art and poetry, it follows two birds as they reflect on the seasons and cycles of change. The title screens are a collaboration with the wonderful multidisciplinary artist and painter Teresa Arega.

The piece was published on “Angry Old Man Magazine”, an online publication for experimental poetry.

  • Released 2018
  • Published in Angry Old Man Magazine
