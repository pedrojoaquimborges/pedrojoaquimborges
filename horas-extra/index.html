DroPe

Mero Acaso is a single off of DroPe’s coming LP record. After geting the rhythm section in place, a big part of the creative mixing process was about developing spaces on which the guest singers’ (Carolina Coutinho and Tiago Sena Silva) voices could exist. I ended up with this kind of 00s pop roomyness that I feel really lets the voices hover to and from the foreground, and contrasts really well with DroPe’s clear, polished and upfront vocal.
