Music Production
Creative partnerships can be a way to break artistic barriers collaboratively. In production, I think they're all about helping artist finding honesty and identity in their work. Diving deeper into their strong points, rather than coming in with pre-made ideas.

Leaping into the artistic process
When producing with another artist, composer or musician, I'm looking at what it is they have in their expression that sheds the most light on their art and thought. Working into that framework is difficult, but most often opens the door for slowly moving towards one's own sound.
Fully hands-on
Collaborating in this way means going all all in creatively. A producer will go with you into the composition, arrangement, writing, sound design, making synth patches or even performing.
