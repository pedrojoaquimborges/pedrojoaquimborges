SHORT BIO

Mixing engineer and musicmaker with a fascination for sounds and their ability to shape presentness and affect in people, beings and places. Born in the atlantic island of Madeira and forever tied to its insularity.

Pedro currently runs a studio in Vilnius, Lithuania, working with artists from various nationalities. In the years prior, he shuffled between audio engineering and undertaking another passion — a small business venture in endogenous food research. After producing and mixing his first record at 16, he pursued musical training both in the academic and professional spheres.

SHOWREEL

I think
extraordinary records
are found within the sounds, not polished onto them. So my process focuses on looking for substance in the music, on magnifying the bits of truthful energy artists planted in their works.

So called “soundgoodizing” and polish are important too. I should say it, since I’ve been trained for that. But great mixes are rooted in long-term decisions that lead artists to flourish with time by learning to connect intention to sonic expression.

I can happily say that a lot of artists I recently worked with have struck record deals, playlist placements and management/promotion offers after our collaboration.
