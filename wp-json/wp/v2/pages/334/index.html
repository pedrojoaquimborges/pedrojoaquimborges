          In some cases, I think Stem mastering can be a great option. I personally treat as a hybrid process, so you can choose to make it either a slightly flexible master, or a final retouching, shaping and balancing of the track’s sonics \u2014 kind of a subtle mix. That last option can be a good choice if you’re a musician mixing your own material.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

          At the moment, I’m running a slightly more affordable pricing on mix\/master\/production services, due to being in the chaotic process of starting to mix and master full-time. On top of that, Covid-19 has taken a heavy toll on many live performers and it feels right to adapt to that. <\/p>\n\n\n\n
