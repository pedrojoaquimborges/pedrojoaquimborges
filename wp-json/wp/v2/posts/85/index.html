Three Poems for Early Spring<\/strong> is a postcard from a short trip to Lisbon. Somewhere between music, sound art and poetry, it follows two birds as they reflect on the seasons and cycles of change. The title screens are a collaboration with the wonderful multidisciplinary artist and painter Teresa Arega. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The piece was published on \u201cAngry Old Man Magazine\u201d, an online publication for experimental poetry.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
