O Ch\u00e3o do Parque<\/strong> is a full-length album dreamed-up by the portuguese composer, rapper and mixing engineer Z\u00e9 Menos. The record is an exceedingly personal art work reflecting on growth, education and life cycles as witnessed by the leaves of a Liquid\u00e2mbar in his park. <\/p>\n\n\n\n

The music\u2019s intimate quality goes down to the technicals – the album being fully conceived by himself, from composition and writing, down to production, mixing and mastering. I\u2019d say I definitely have a penchant for music works with this authorship quality. Despite my respect for the powerhouse that the creative\/production cycle of a song can be, I deeply feel like there is a very performative aspect to pouring one\u2019s artistry into every aspect of a work\u2019s production – the results tend to have a grandeur that really affirms recorded music as an artform, rather than a supporting act to gigs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Its physical form is a small precious book that features beautiful artwork by multidisciplinary artist Teresa Arega and a single Liquid\u00e2mbar leaf.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
